PREDICTING MOVIE SUCCESS!

If you love cinema, and you are constantly looking for the best ways to keep up to date with what's new and what's upcoming, then this site is going to be perfect for you to predict the success of the movie before the release of movie.

OVERVIEW


In today's world, the film industry and technology are developing significantly. Whether one watches the movie on mobile or in the theatre, it is really important to know what kind of movies people like which will eventually help the producers and directors to make great movies. Also, it will help a lot of cinema hall owners as they need to manage screening for the movie. So we are fetching comments and tweets from YouTube and Twitter API respectively after the release of the trailer of the movie and performing Sentiment analysis on them. Thus, we propose a system to predict box-office movie success using machine learning concepts by analyzing the sent. We use some algorithms like decision tree regression, SVR and random forest regression to predict due to weight age and then based on multiple thresholds calculated on the basis of descriptive statistics of dataset of each component it is given class flop, hit, super hit label.
